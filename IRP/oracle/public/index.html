What is Sentra?

We could simply tell you, but it wouldn't make sense yet. Sentra adapts—the more you share about your work, your interests, your challenges, the better Sentra can demonstrate what it actually does. Each conversation is different because each person is different. We are genuinely curious to learn about your world!

Please note: This is an experimental AI system for artistic purposes. Don't share sensitive personal information, financial details, or private data. Conversations may be used for academic research. This system is not intended for medical, legal, or professional advice.
