Abstract

Occult of Encryption is a publication//visual essay on the modalities of interaction with AI and data safeguarding techniques. Through its discourse, the work investigates the critical climate of data safety in regards to usage of generative AI. Testing and expanding the framework of Data Trust, the project aims to encourage the readers to reflect on usage of AI in relation to their own creativity, as well as the creativity of others. By fostering exploration, the work aims to reshape how AI is perceived and integrated, highlighting {ethical considerations + its potential as a creative tool}.

The race to document and make sense of AI’s rapid development is an inherently lost battle for its constant evolution and acceleration. The deliberate choice of print medium for this publication serves as a non-changeable testimonial of a specific.moment {time*place}. Unlike digital formats that can be endlessly updated, print provides a static and grounded resource, preserving insights and discourse relevant at the time of publication, serving as a time capsule.

Introduction

Technology’s exponential integration into modern life has transformed it from an optional tool into an essential infrastructure. What once was framed as innovation of convenience, now is firmly embedded into the contemporary technological rituals of the modern society. Society and computation, both shaping and being shaped by one another, blur the boundaries of influence to such an extent that it becomes difficult to discern where necessity ends and manufactured demand begins.

Contemporary design transcends the isolated mediums, extending into meta- forms of crafting networks, systems, interfaces and even ourselves. Arguably, there is no longer an “outside” to design—the world itself has been designed. In the data-dialogue, interaction modalities {interfaces} thus becomes the mediums turning the temporal experience into narrative devices. Through such paradigm, the digital footprints are not just byproducts of interaction but the very driver or a condition of initiated variable. From this landscape emerges datafication - paradigm in which the interactions with the interfaces generate data which is being gathered and processed:

Data Safeguarding

With the release of Public AI, the discourse around data complicates further. LLMS and GenAI rely on vast datasets for training and for calibration. Billions of imagery scraped all over internet - traces of our collective digital footprint are ingrained into these models. The commofified nature of these models enforces the opaque modality of ineractions - we engage with the outputs of such without insight into underlying processes that shape them. Illustrated in the diagram below, the modality of interaction has an ambiguous input, a concealed process, and a visible output.

As practitioners exploring the role of generative AI, it is essential to reject the surface level modality of interaction that are proposed in commodified nature. Using AI in meaningful purposes requires going beyond, demystifying and deconstructing.

Closed and Open Systems for AI

Cloud and Open systems like Midjourney/Open AI provide implicit disclaimers on training data. The reality is that both of the prompts and the outputs will be used in training and fine-tuning later version of the models deployed - inputs and outputs are fed back into the system. Closed and Local Systems operate within a secure close network therefore minimising all of the data leakage.

Data Trust Applied

FutureArtsEcosystem provides a framework to address data safety when working with AI. Report on the AI and Public Art, it was proposed for a new direction of artistry to be custom model making when artists would train their own local model. The framework to achieve such can be viewed as Data Trust model - Intersectional work between Data Subject, Data Stewart and Fascilitating Body..

The framework from FAE is expanded and applied to fasciliating work beween artist and AI through an intermediatery in the context of utilising AI ethically and thoughtfully as a creative tool (notcustom model making). In thus context, the Data Steward role is guiding the data safety of existing work as well as artistic intent, avoiding data leakage and providing a safety system when working with AI. Data Subject trusts Data Steward to guard their data.
